THE DOROTHY J. HENDERSON REVOCABLE LIVING TRUST

THIS TRUST AGREEMENT is made this ____ day of _______, 2022, between DOROTHY JUNE HENDERSON, as Grantor and Trustee.

ARTICLE I
TRUST ESTATE

The Grantor hereby transfers and delivers to the Trustee the property described in Schedule A attached hereto, receipt of which is hereby acknowledged by the Trustee, to have and to hold, together with all additions thereto, upon the terms and conditions hereinafter set forth.

ARTICLE II
DISPOSITIVE PROVISIONS

A. During Grantor's Lifetime
During the lifetime of the Grantor, the Trustee shall pay to or apply for the benefit of the Grantor all of the net income of the Trust Estate, and such portions of the principal as the Grantor may request in writing.

B. Upon Grantor's Death
Upon the death of the Grantor, the Trustee shall distribute the Trust Estate as follows:

1. Personal Effects and Tangible Personal Property
All personal effects, household furnishings, and artwork shall be distributed in accordance with any written memorandum left by the Grantor, or if no such memorandum exists, in equal shares to WILLIAM CHARLES HENDERSON and PATRICIA HENDERSON-DAVIS.

2. Specific Distributions
a) The sum of FIFTY THOUSAND DOLLARS ($50,000) to The Ridgefield Arts Council
b) The sum of TWENTY-FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS ($25,000) to each grandchild of the Grantor living at the time of Grantor's death

3. Residuary Trust Estate
The remainder of the Trust Estate shall be divided into equal shares for the Grantor's children, WILLIAM CHARLES HENDERSON and PATRICIA HENDERSON-DAVIS.

ARTICLE III
TRUSTEE PROVISIONS

A. Successor Trustee
Upon the death, resignation, or incapacity of the Grantor as Trustee, WILLIAM CHARLES HENDERSON shall serve as Successor Trustee.

ARTICLE IV
ADMINISTRATIVE PROVISIONS

The Trustee shall have all powers granted by Connecticut law, including but not limited to the power to:
- Invest and reinvest trust assets
- Buy, sell, and manage real property
- Make distributions for health, education, maintenance, and support
- Employ professional advisors

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Trust Agreement on the date first above written.
