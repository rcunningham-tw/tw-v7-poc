LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT

I, Margaret Elizabeth Thompson, residing at 2847 Willow Creek Drive, Fairfield, Connecticut, being of sound mind and disposing memory, do hereby make, publish and declare this to be my Last Will and Testament, hereby revoking all wills and codicils previously made by me.

ARTICLE I: EXECUTOR
I hereby appoint my daughter, Sarah Thompson-Mitchell, as Executor of this Will. If she is unable or unwilling to serve, I appoint my son, Michael Robert Thompson, as alternate Executor.

ARTICLE II: PAYMENT OF DEBTS AND EXPENSES
I direct my Executor to pay all my just debts, funeral expenses, and expenses of administration as soon as practicable after my death.

ARTICLE III: SPECIFIC BEQUESTS
1. I give and bequeath my jewelry collection to my daughter, Sarah Thompson-Mitchell.
2. I give and bequeath my 2019 Mercedes-Benz E-Class to my son, Michael Robert Thompson.
3. I give and bequeath the sum of $25,000 to St. Mary's Catholic Church, Fairfield, Connecticut.

ARTICLE IV: RESIDUARY ESTATE
All the rest, residue, and remainder of my estate, both real and personal, of whatever kind and wherever situated, I give, devise, and bequeath in equal shares to my children, Sarah Thompson-Mitchell and Michael Robert Thompson, or to the survivor of them.

ARTICLE V: GUARDIAN
In the event that any beneficiary under this Will is a minor at the time of my death, I nominate Patricia Henderson-Davis as guardian of such minor's person and property.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and seal this _____ day of _________, 2023.

Margaret Elizabeth Thompson
